The Science Of Timing

Why start early?

The right brain is dominant between ages 0 and 7. At around age 3, dominance begins shifting to the left brain. Earlier stimulation means deeper capability.

Right → Left

Ages 0–7 is the right-brain period. The brain develops from right to left.

Diminishing ability

A child's magical ability diminishes if not stimulated early.

Earlier = deeper

A 2-year-old learns faster than a 3-year-old. Earlier means deeper capability.
